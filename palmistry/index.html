About the Research Lab

The Palmistry Lab is a research-grade project under Sathyadarshana, uniting ancient Hasta Sastra wisdom with modern computer vision and AI-based analysis.

This platform captures hand images, highlights the seven major lines, and interprets them using advanced algorithms and the KP astrology model.

Features

  • 📸 Real-time camera capture & analysis (Analyzer page only)
  • 🌈 7-line color classification (Heart, Head, Life, etc.)
  • 🧠 Integration with KP Astrology module
  • 📜 AI-generated insight reports (PDF export)
  • 🌐 Works offline (PWA compatible)
